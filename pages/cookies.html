Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies primarily for authentication purposes. When you log in, we store a token in your browser's local storage or cookies to keep you logged in as you navigate between pages.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our externa API providers (TMDB, Jikan, RAWG) do not set cookies on your browser through our site.

4. Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that this may affect your ability to log in and use our services.
